The best video platforms To sell your content on...

There are actualy lots of different video platforms where you can upload your videos and get paid directly by the viewer for your content. Lets take a look and see what they each offer regarding fees, commission, premiums etc.

Shabingo.com

Shabingo.com is a free service to sign up to. The only fee you have to pay is a flat commission rate of 23% of any sale you make. How it works is you upload a video with a preview. When people view the preview they are asked at the end of the preview if they want to watch the full Shabingo. Now the cool thing about Shabingo is that you have a choice if you want to watch the whole video... You can either pay the nominal price to watch the video or you can share the video on either facebook or twitter. Whats cool about that for an uploader is that if they coose to pay, well thats great you get paid and if they decide to share well depending on how many facebook friends or twitter followers they have your video is now shared into hundreds or possibly thousands of social media feeds from one click


GUMROAD

What gumroad does is it provides you with the tools to get paid for any digital content. So it could be e-books , images, videos etc. They provide you with the tools but do not provide you with any traffic. That is up to you. It is good for using it in conjunction with your existing site.


Pivotshare

Pivotshare allows you to create your own subscription video on demand channel. Again you have to ge your own traffic. They take a commission of 30%


Vimeo

Vimeo lets you put a tip jar with your videos if you sign u to their on demand premium service which will cost you $199 plus 10% commission. Your account also has to be in good standing according to their guide lines.


StreamingVideosOnline

StreamingVideosOnline allows you to sell videos online from your own website. Prices range from $15 per month up to $1299 per month.
